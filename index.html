About us

Why a Coffee Roaster in Seward, Alaska?

Alaskans are lucky in our coffee choices, but our founders, Sadie and Julian, felt the Kenai Peninsula needed our own, local coffee roaster. When our roasting facility expanded to included a coffee shop, we wanted to offer coffee brewed with exceptional consistency and quality in a cozy, community-focused space.

Our menu includes vegan, gluten-free, and dairy free options year-round. Come see us in historic downtown Seward and see why thousands of locals and visitors alike choose Resurrection Bay Coffee again and again.
